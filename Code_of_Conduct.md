## CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md

### Our Commitment

We are committed to a professional, respectful, and thoughtful environment.

This project operates at the intersection of technology, society, and ethics. Discussions may be intense, but **respect is mandatory**.

---

### Expected Behavior

* Respect differing viewpoints
* Critique ideas, not people
* Be precise and honest
* Avoid hype-driven or manipulative language

---

### Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment or personal attacks
* Deliberate misinformation
* Attempts to derail ethical discussions
* Promotion of harmful or exploitative uses of AI

---

### Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to:

* Remove comments or contributions
* Temporarily or permanently restrict participation

Decisions are guided by the long-term health of the project, not popularity.

---

### Final Note

If you are looking to build uncontrolled, extractive, or opaque AI systems, this project is likely **not** for you.
