* R1 – Minimal
* R2 – Moderate
* R3 – High
* R4 – Systemic
* R5 – Existential

### 3. Existential Risk Category

Includes:

* Weaponization
* Centralized authoritarian capture
* Self-amplifying misinformation loops
* Systemic destabilization of civil society

All R5 risks require:

* Multi-node validation
* Human constitutional council approval
* Immutable log recording

* Continuous automated scoring
* Quarterly human audit
